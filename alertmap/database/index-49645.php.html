Rapid Information System

Rapid Information Service (EDIS RIS) a comprehensive suite of emergency notification services. Our reliable and cost-effective mass notification solutions help people to connect during a crisis, manage operational incidents and other communication activities.

The RIS is an easy-to-use, web-based emergency notification system that enables effective, real-time email messaging. Our mass notification and management services are ideal for government agencies, municipalities, non-profit organizations, international organozations and first-responders.

This service is available only to government, international organizations, military service and non-governmental organizations. Thank you for your understanding. We are a small business, which hasn't government or other outside funding, and we appreciate every partners.

If you need a REST-API or Common Alerting Protocol (paid, business-based) service, please contact us.
