Risk Analisys "I will travel to...."

If you have travelling plans, on this page you can check if RSOE EDIS has registered any event in that area in the past 3 days. After reviewing these events carefully, you can make a decision on whether or not it is safe to travel to the country of your choice. This service is free of charge in its current form.

Attention! Even if this service doesn't show any possible risk factors, there could be events in the country that we don't have information about or that simply don't meet the threshold from which EDIS registers the event. Please check out other information sources as well before the trip.
